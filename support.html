Support

Need help with HIIT Timer?

If something doesn’t work as expected or you have a suggestion, we’d love to hear from you. The app is intentionally simple, so most issues can be solved in a couple of quick steps.

Common questions

  • Timer not starting? Make sure you have set both work and rest durations, then tap the main Start button.
  • No sound or haptics? Check your iPhone’s mute switch, volume level, and that “Do Not Disturb” / Focus modes are not blocking notifications.
  • Screen turning off? Keep the screen awake by starting a session – the app is designed to stay on during an active workout.

Contact us

For bugs, feedback or feature requests, email us with as much detail as you can:

Email: support@yourhiittimerapp.com

If you’re reporting a bug, it helps if you include:

  • Your iPhone model (e.g. iPhone 15).
  • Your iOS version (e.g. iOS 18.1).
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if possible.
